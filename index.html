About 01

01coin is an inoovative, cryptographically-secured digital asset ledger based on blockchain technology. Developed in part from the core code behind Dash, 01coin includes many innovative enhancements.

01coin aims to become the most community-centric project in all of crypto. Born out of its own ashes when the orignal development team mysteriously disappeared one month after the coin launched, it is now completely managed and operated by the community.
